Our Story

A Quiet Place for Reflection

At Bright Haven Publishing, we create uplifting coloring books that celebrate faith, beauty, and creativity. Each page invites quiet attention and contemplation.

Our temple coloring series is a tribute to sacred architecture and the peace that comes from embracing times of mindful reflection. Our scripture coloring books walk readers through the Old Testament, the Book of Mormon, and the season of Advent one page at a time, leaving room for discussing and teaching about moments in the scriptures.

We believe creative practice is a form of devotion, and that beautifully made books can become a small daily haven in a hurried world.
