About the press

Coloring books and other small wonders.

Dropthorn Press makes coloring books, activity books, and gentle little reads for kids of all ages, adults, and seniors. Most of our catalog belongs to the Ultimate Bold and Easy Coloring Books series, designed for a relaxing experience without the frustration of microscopic detail.

Themes range from nature and animals to seasonal celebrations and American history. We make books you can finish, dog-ear, and come back to.
