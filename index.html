a little about us

We're a small group who really like books.

Page & Path Publishing Group is the parent of three indie imprints. We're not big. We don't try to be. We pick our covers, watch our paper stock, and obsess about how a book actually feels when you sit with it.

Each imprint has its own audience and its own voice. What they share is this: every title has been thought about by an actual person who wanted it to exist.

what we ask of every book

The three things that matter.

  • Is it beautifully made? Paper, print, layout, the whole feel of holding it.
  • Will it actually do what it says? Help you unwind, focus, reflect, or play.
  • Will it be worth coming back to? One use isn't enough; we want it on your shelf.
