About Page & Path

We are a publishing collective devoted to creating thoughtfully designed creative outlets for people of all ages and interests.

Our Mission

Page & Path Publishing Group is home to three distinctive publishing imprints, each with its own voice, audience, and creative vision. We believe people deserve books that are as beautifully made as they are enjoyable—whether those books challenge the mind, provide a creative escape, or inspire reflection.

We bring together publishers who share a commitment to quality design, intentional content, and respect for your time.

What We Do

Our imprints publish in many categories: brain-training puzzles and journals, bold and easy coloring books, and journals for reflection and devotion. But across every genre, we ask the same questions:

  • Is this book beautifully made?
  • Will it actually help you unwind or engage?
  • Will it still be worth coming back to?
